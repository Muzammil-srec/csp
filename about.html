Why We Did This

This CSP project investigates the drinking water conditions in rural areas. We studied purification systems, storage tanks, and real-life usage in village homes.

What We Found

Even with infrastructure in place, proper maintenance, awareness, and clean storage were lacking. Our flame of passion was lit when we saw people unaware of the dangers of contaminated water.

Our Mission

Like a Demon Slayer with purpose, we aim to eliminate ignorance and spread awareness about safe drinking water. Our project proposes both technical and community-based solutions for healthier villages.
